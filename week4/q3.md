# Indian IT Act

## Key Events
- **1996:** UNCITRAL Model Law on Electronic Commerce is adopted, providing the global template for IT laws.
- **June 9, 2000:** The Information Technology Act (2000) is enacted in India.
- **October 17, 2000:** The IT Act 2000 officially comes into force.
- **2008:** Major Amendments are introduced to include issues like cyber-terrorism and data protection.
- **2015:** The Supreme Court strikes down Section 66A (Shreya Singhal v. Union of India) due to its impact on free speech.
